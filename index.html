Background

The Foundation in Lucknow

My perspective as a creator was born in Lucknow, a city that provided the backdrop for my earliest encounters with technology. Born on April 27, 2010, I spent my early childhood at The Tree House, where the first seeds of curiosity were planted. This environment fostered a natural inclination toward understanding the mechanics of the world around me, setting the stage for a life defined by technical and artistic inquiry.

The years between 2014 and 2021 at St. Fidelis College were essential in shaping my academic discipline. It was here that I began to move beyond simple curiosity and started exploring the digital landscape as a serious medium. This period was marked by a steady transition from being a consumer of digital content to becoming a student of its architecture.

The Stack

C# and Systems Logic

My approach to programming is rooted in the architecture of C#. I view code as the skeletal system of any digital experience, requiring both flexibility and strength. I specialize in building modular engine components and systemic gameplay modules that can scale without friction.

Advanced 3D in Blender

In the realm of Blender, my work focuses on the philosophy of hard surface modeling. I prioritize clean topology and procedural workflows, ensuring that every asset is optimized for real-time rendering without sacrificing visual fidelity.

Anomalies

The Experimental Log

Beyond the structured world of design pipelines, I have always been driven by a "break it to understand it" mentality. One of my most persistent challenges was turning an aging laptop into a functioning Hackintosh, fighting against hardware limitations to force an operating system into a space it was never meant to occupy.

Not every experiment has been a quiet technical success. There was a notable instance where my curiosity regarding electrical load led to short circuiting my entire room. While it was a momentarily startling event, it served as a very practical introduction to the fundamental laws of electricity.

I have also spent time building custom hardware, such as assembling a functional MP3 player from scratch. Projects like these allow me to appreciate the tactile side of technology, stepping away from the screen to work with raw, physical circuit boards.

Experience

Creative Direction

Currently, my focus is on Creative Direction. I am no longer just building components, I am architecting entire experiences. By merging my background in cinematography with my technical engineering skills, I am building a portfolio of work that pushes the boundaries of digital storytelling.
