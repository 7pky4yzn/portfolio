Background

The Foundation in Lucknow

My perspective as a creator was born in Lucknow, a city that provided the backdrop for my earliest encounters with technology. Born on April 27, 2010, I spent my early childhood at The Tree House, where the first seeds of curiosity were planted.

The years between 2014 and 2021 at St. Fidelis College were essential in shaping my academic discipline. This period was marked by a steady transition from being a consumer of digital content to becoming a student of its architecture.

Currently, I am navigating the intersection of engineering and design at Sunbeam School in Robertsganj. This chapter is focused on the rigorous application of physics and mathematics to creative problems.
