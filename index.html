The Narrative

I build because creating feels instinctive to me. I’m driven by the urge to turn abstract thoughts into things that work, feel intentional, and last. Whether it’s code, design, or sound, I’m always searching for clarity—stripping ideas down until what remains feels obvious and honest.

I think deeply about systems and details. Small decisions matter to me because they shape how something is experienced over time. I value ownership, independence, and work that respects the user rather than chasing attention or trends. Emotion and logic coexist in how I approach problems—structure gives form, but intent gives meaning.

I learn by doing, experimenting, and iterating. I’m comfortable figuring things out without a map, refining slowly instead of rushing for recognition. I prefer depth over noise, timelessness over novelty, and work that speaks quietly but confidently for itself.

Competencies

My technical foundation is built on full-stack architecture and creative engineering. I specialize in bridging the gap between complex backend logic and fluid, high-fidelity interfaces that feel alive.

In the realm of design, I focus on visual systems and motion. I believe interaction is a conversation, and every micro-transition should guide the user with purpose and rhythmic elegance.

Beyond execution, I am a systems thinker. I enjoy optimizing workflows and building scalable environments where code and design don't just coexist, but amplify each other's impact.

Instruments

My primary stack revolves around React, TypeScript, and Node.js, often utilizing Framer Motion or GSAP to bring physical properties to digital surfaces.

For design and prototyping, I rely on Figma and Cinema 4D. I treat the canvas like a workshop, where lighting, material, and space are just as important as the code that follows.

I am constantly evolving my toolkit, currently exploring WebGPU and Rust to push the boundaries of what is possible in real-time browser-based experiences.
